Welding is not just a profession; it’s an art that builds the future

Welding is a process used to join materials, typically metals or thermoplastics, by causing coalescence. This is usually achieved by melting the workpieces and adding a filler material to form a strong joint. The choice of welding method depends on factors like the materials being welded, the desired strength, and the application's requirements.
